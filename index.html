Places Must Visit

Bicol

I visited bicol in september 2024

I went to Bicol with my family during September, to meet friends and organize a Christian based camp there. Bicol is a very beautiful place, golden sunsets, fine sand, and warm beach water! You can see many fishes, if you choose to snorkel. Over all, Bicol is a wonderful province to stay and have a vacation in.



Places Must Visit






Hungduan Ifugao

I visited Hungduan,Ifugao in May 2024

I went to Hungduan,Ifugao with my family during May, to organize a Christian based camp there. Hungduan, Ifugao is VERY UNDERATED! It is a very beautiful place! Fun fact about Hungduan, Ifugao has a hospital. Why fun fact? Because,the hospital is called Good News hospital, that hospital was passed down generation after generation. The founder gave up EVERYTHING to build a hospital for the locals. You can go to their Facebook page to support them more. Their Facebook page "https://www.facebook.com/pages/Good-News-Clinic-Hospital/513257085362305" You can copy paste it and you will find their Facebook page. Anyway, Hungduan, Ifugao has beautiful cool mountains, which REALLY help during the hot summers here in the Philippines. Hungduan, Ifugao has very beautiful rice terraces made ONLY BY HAND!Over all, Hungduan, Ifugao is a beautiful and wonderous place.
